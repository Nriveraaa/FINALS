About Us

Welcome to CHARLIETA! We are an online shop dedicated to providing you with stylish, comfortable, and affordable items. Our mission is to bring you high-quality products at low prices, making it feel like a thrift store but online.

My name is Nicole Rivera, the founder of CHARLIETA, and I am 20 years old. I started this online shop to offer a wide range of products that cater to your needs and preferences without breaking the bank. At CHARLIETA, we believe in the importance of quality and customer satisfaction.


Our Story

CHARLIETA was founded with a simple idea: to create a one-stop shop where you can find everything you need in one place at affordable prices. We started as a small team with a big dream, and over the years, we have grown into a trusted online retailer known for our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.

Our journey has been driven by our passion for innovation and excellence. We continuously strive to improve and expand our product offerings to meet the ever-changing needs of our customers. From our humble beginnings to becoming a go-to destination for quality products, our story is one of dedication, hard work, and unwavering commitment to our customers.


Our Vision

Our vision is to become a leading online retailer known for its commitment to quality, affordability, and customer satisfaction. We aim to create a seamless shopping experience for our customers, offering a diverse range of products that cater to all tastes and preferences. We strive to be a brand that our customers can trust and rely on for all their shopping needs.


Our Values

  • Quality: We ensure that all our products are made from high-quality materials and meet our strict standards.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Our customers are at the heart of everything we do. We are dedicated to providing excellent service and ensuring that you are happy with your purchases.
  • Innovation: We are always looking for new and innovative ways to improve our products and services.
  • Integrity: We conduct our business with honesty and transparency, building trust with our customers.

We hope you enjoy shopping with us as much as we enjoy bringing our products to you. If you have any questions or feedback, please do not hesitate to contact us.
